SOLAR FACILITY DETAILS:
-----------------------
Project Name: Whatcom County Solar Farm Phase II
Location: Ferndale, WA
System Size: 2.5 MW (Community Allocation: 500 kW)
Expected Annual Generation: 625,000 kWh
Technology: Monocrystalline Silicon Panels with Single-axis Tracking

PRICING STRUCTURE:
-----------------
Base Rate: $0.085 per kWh (Year 1)
Annual Escalation: 2.9% per year
Subscriber Discount: 15% below retail utility rate
Guaranteed Savings: Minimum 10% annually vs. PSE standard rate

PAYMENT TERMS:
-------------
Billing Cycle: Monthly
Payment Due: Net 30 days
Late Fee: 1.5% per month on overdue amounts
Security Deposit: $2,500 (refundable after 12 months good standing)

ALLOCATION & CREDITS:
--------------------
Community Share: 20% of total facility output
Monthly Allocation: ~52,083 kWh (estimated)
Credit Mechanism: Net metering credits applied to member utility bills
Excess Credit Handling: Carried forward up to 12 months

PERFORMANCE GUARANTEES:
----------------------
Annual Production Guarantee: 90% of estimated generation
Performance Monitoring: Real-time via web portal
Maintenance: Included in PPA rate
Equipment Warranty: 25 years panels, 20 years inverters

TERMS & CONDITIONS:
------------------
Contract Term: 25 years from Commercial Operation Date
Early Termination: Permitted with 180-day notice + termination fee
Termination Fee Schedule:
- Years 1-5: $15,000
- Years 6-10: $10,000
- Years 11-15: $5,000
- Years 16-25: No fee

SUBSCRIBER RESPONSIBILITIES:
---------------------------
- Maintain good standing with local utility
- Provide 60-day notice for any changes in utility account
- Participate in quarterly community meetings
- Comply with utility interconnection requirements

RENEWABLE ENERGY CERTIFICATES (RECs):
------------------------------------
REC Ownership: Retained by Solar Provider for first 10 years
REC Transfer: RECs transfer to community after Year 10
Environmental Benefits: Subscriber may claim carbon offset benefits

INSURANCE & LIABILITY:
---------------------
General Liability: $2,000,000 per occurrence
Property Insurance: Full replacement value of equipment
Subscriber Liability: Limited to monthly subscription amount

DISPUTE RESOLUTION:
------------------
Governing Law: State of Washington
Dispute Process: Mediation first, then binding arbitration
Venue: King County Superior Court
Attorney Fees: Prevailing party entitled to reasonable fees

SPECIAL PROVISIONS:
------------------
Community Expansion: Right of first refusal for additional capacity
Technology Upgrades: Cost-neutral improvements included at no charge
Force Majeure: Standard provisions for uncontrollable events
Regulatory Changes: Good faith renegotiation if laws change materially
